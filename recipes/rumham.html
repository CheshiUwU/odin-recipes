Rum Ham

Delightful ham with hints of rum.

Rum Ham is a ham that has been steeped and injected with a rum brine, and coated with a rum glaze.

Ingredients:

  • ham
  • rum (one 750Ml bottle)
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 1/4 - 3/4 cup molasses
  • 1 cup salt
  • 2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 1 stick of butter
  • Optional Ingredients:
    • Peppercorn
    • Cloves
    • Bay Leaf
  • Equipment Needed:
    • Baking Dish
    • Cooking Thermometer
    • Cutting Board

Cooking Steps:

  1. Gather ingredients and equipment
  2. Make the brine
  3. Steep ham in brine
  4. Glaze the ham
  5. Serve
